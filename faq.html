Frequently Asked Questions

If you are unsure about something, you can find answers to some frequently asked questions below. However, if you still can't find an answer to your question, don't hesitate to contact us

Regarding the process

What is the process of building my site?

To place an order, visit our contacts page. Within 48 hours, you'll be contacted by one of our representatives, with whom you'll be able to discuss your needs and wishes.
During the process of development, you may be contacted by our representative to give your opinion on certain matters. You are also encouraged to contact our representative if you require clarification for something.
After the site itself is ready, some features like SEO might still need development, as they require collecting data over multiple months

How long will it take to build my website?

It largely depends on the complexity of the features you require. A static site may be shipped within 2 weeks, while a site with sophisticated back-end features that require testing and security patches may be under development for months. You can count on our representative giving you a rough time estimate upon assesing the project.

Will you handle the design or do I need to provide my own design?

You may provide a design if you wish, but our design team will handle it otherwise.

Regarding support

Will you make revisions if I want to?

If you paid for the product in full, we offer 6 months of maintenance and revisions. If you opted for an extended payment plan, we offer maintenance and revisions for the course of the entire payment duration. If you are still eligible for revisions, you can contact our representative and inform them of the required change. Keep in mind that some changes may be impossible to implement, or others may require futher payment

Will you handle hosting?

If you paid for the product in full, we offer 6 months of hosting. If you opted for an extended payment plan, we offer hosting for the course of the entire payment duration.

Will you provide a domain or do I need to get one myself?

We will acquire a custom domain of your choice for a period of 12 months. If you already own a domain though, you are free to use that one

Regarding pricing

How much will my website cost?

You can contact us, and within 48 hours we can arrange an online meeting to discuss your specific needs. Afterwards, we will offer an estimate for the price
