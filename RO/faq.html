Frequently Asked Questions

Regarding the process

What is the process of building my site?

After placing your order on our website, we will contact you through your prefered method and confirm the order. Before the development process starts, we will require that you pay a deposit worth one month of your selected plan to ensure your intentions are truthful. If you wish to cancel the deal at any time before the site is completed, contact us and 100% of your deposit will be returned promptly.
Within 48 hours, you will be redirected to one of our developers who will work on your site. They will listen to your needs and ask questions to understand what the website should look like and what features you would like.
During the development process you may be contacted by your developer to make decisions about development. You are also free to contact them at any time if you wish to clarify something or ask a question.
After the website is finished, it's ready to be published online.However, some features, like SEO, will be worked upon for multiple months after your website is public. Your deposit will now be credited towards your website payments, effectively covering your first billing month.

How long will it take to build my website?

It largely depends on the complexity of the features you require. A static site may be shipped within 2 weeks, while a site with sophisticated back-end features that require testing and security patches may be under development for months. You can count on your designated developer giving you a rough time estimate upon assesing the project.

Will you handle the design or do I need to provide my own design?

You may provide a design if you wish, but we will handle it otherwise.

Regarding support

What kind of support do you offer after the website is launched?

Regardless of your chosen subscription plan, we will fully maintain your website, and will update it with any changes you may desire.

Will you make changes to the site after it's launched if I want to?

After the launch of the site, you can contact your designated developer at any moment and let them know whenever you wish to make any changes to your site. Keep in mind that not all changes might be possible to implement, or you may require to upgrade your plan for especially complex features

Will you handle the website hosting or do I need to find my own?

Every plan comes with website hosting included, no extra charges required.

Will you provide a domain or do I need to get one myself?

Regardless of your chosen plan, we will provide a custom domain to your liking for your website and renew it yearly without any extra charges. If you wish to use a domain you already have ownership of, you are free to do so.

Regarding pricing

How much will my website cost?

We offer 3 different price points, each cattered to different needs. Check out our pricing page and decide which plan is best for you.

What if I wish to switch my plan ?

If you wish to change your plan, we recommend contacting your designated developer and decide whether you actually need to change your plan or not. If you decide to go through with your decision, your developer will work with you to fullfil your wish. The changes to the subscription fee will be reflected from the next billing month forward.

What if I wish to cancel my plan ?

If you wish to cancel your plan, contact your designated developer to let them know you wish to cancel your plan, and by the start of the next billing month, your website will be taken down. Keep in mind that you will no longer be entitled to any of our services, and we hold no more responsabilities over your online presence.

Can I buy the website instead of paying a subscription fee each month?

Yes, you can. If you choose to do so, however, we will provide support and website hosting for only 3 months after the transaction, and domain renewal for only one year. Buying the website costs 8 months-worth of what your selected plan costs (e.g. If you chose the Value plan, which costs $x/month, buying the site will cost $8x). If you change your mind at any time after the transaction and wish to receive our service once again, you must void your rights over the source-code, assets and design.
